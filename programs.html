Our Programs

The Teshie Orphanage has dedicated itself to supporting orphans, underprivileged children, teenage mothers, and the elderly, providing education, vocational training, and medical care. Since its founding, it has made significant strides, including enrolling children in schools, supporting vocational apprenticeships, and celebrating tertiary education achievements. By 2015, it had matured into a fully-fledged humanitarian service, committed to ensuring all children enjoy a happy, healthy, and secure childhood. The Home continues to expand its efforts, fostering collaborations to provide well-rounded education and career opportunities for all beneficiaries.
